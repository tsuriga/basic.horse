- What is Basic Horse? -
Basic Horse doesn't make underwear, they create games and software. This group/collective was founded by two guys; Olli Suoranta and Juho Saarelainen, sharing the same interest towards polished and good sounding virtual experiences which are easy to learn but hard to master. Both using Linux operating systems, faschinated by web development and getting the ideology behind open-source software development..

Olli Suoranta (http://allformsofpandas.com)
- Multi-instrumentalist, software engineer with common knowledge about visual arts. Works during daytime in the realms of e-commerce and after the sunset juggles with all kinds of creative stuff e.g. music and sounds, pixel art and games. Is the main founder of Basic Horse and the foolish visionary leading the projects..

Juho Saarelainen (https://github.com/saarelaine)
- Automation Engineer, all around excellent guy with knowledge spanning over various fields. Enjoys hanging around the more complex functions in Basic Horse projects such as raycasting and z-indexing. Is the co-founder of Basic Horse and happens to be the designer of the Logo.
